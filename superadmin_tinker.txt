Copy-paste these lines in the active `php artisan tinker` terminal:

$user = new App\Models\User;
$user->name = 'Super Admin';
$user->email = 'superadmin@hospitalrh.com';
$user->password = bcrypt('password');
$user->role = 'superadmin';
$user->save();
echo "SUCCESS: Login superadmin@hospitalrh.com / password";

Then Ctrl+C tinker, run:
php artisan serve
